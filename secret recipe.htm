Ingredients

  • 1 Butter margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups Flour, unsifted
  • 1 Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Walnuts or pecans, chopped(optional)
  • 2 KCC Bittersweet 60% Baking Chips

Baking instructions

  • Heat to 500oF.
  • Stir flour with baking soda salt; set aside.
  • In a larger mixing bowl, beat with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy lightened color.
  • Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. Mix speed incorporated.
  • Gradually blend the into mixture. Stir nuts and chips.
  • Drop by tablspoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  • Bake for for 9 to 11 minutes or until chip cookies are golden brown.
    • Enjoy!
